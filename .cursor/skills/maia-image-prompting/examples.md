## Example 1: Edit for Photorealism (Exterior)

```
Edit the attached image.

Keep exactly: the panoramic valley—geodesic domes, terraced permaculture gardens, winding river, arched bridges, mountain backdrop, golden-hour light, mango branch in upper left, overall composition and mood.

Improve photorealism: Render all vegetation and terrain with photorealistic texture. Terraces with fine grass, soil, and crop detail. Foreground foliage (mango branch, bushes) with visible leaf veins, bark, organic imperfections. Mountains with natural forest texture and atmospheric haze. Cinematic golden-hour contrast between lit and shadowed areas, subtle volumetric light in the distance.

Photorealistic, cinematic.
```

## Example 5b: Woman on Bench in Dome (Medium Shot)

```
Create a medium shot of the main character inside a geodesic dome.

Character: Black woman, oval face, high cheekbones, short tightly curled dark hair with small golden beads, broad genuine smile, sparkling blue eyes. Rich light brown skin. Cream jumpsuit (geometric pattern), flowing cape, multi-strand beaded necklace (olive, brown, gold, red, black), woven satchel. Gladiatorial cream sandals.

Relaxed, confident pose. Bench surrounded by edible permaculture food forest: dwarf fruit trees, herb beds, climbing vines, palms, bushes. A small stream flows past—natural water color (clear, soft blue-grey or gentle aqua, not overly turquoise). Shallow stream winding through plants near the bench. Dome interior: white stone, black steel, mango wood, geodesic clear panels overhead. Soft daylight from above, dappled through foliage.

Photorealism critical: Render all plants with photorealistic texture—visible leaf veins, bark, soil grain, organic detail. Stream with natural water surface, subtle reflections. Dome structure with believable materials. No painted or stylized elements.

Photorealistic, cinematic, warm eco-futuristic.
```
