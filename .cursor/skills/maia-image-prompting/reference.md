# MaiaOS Image World Reference

Complete reference for consistent image generation and editing across all MaiaOS scenes.

---

## Main Character

**Appearance**
- Black woman
- Sparkling blue eyes
- Short, tightly curled dark hair
- Subtle gold accents or small beads in hair
- Rich light brown skin tone
- Confident, genuine smile

**Clothing (consistent character reference)**

- **Jumpsuit:** Form-fitting, sleeveless or long-sleeved, V-neck in light cream or off-white. Subtle embossed or woven geometric pattern (diamonds, chevrons) across torso and legs. Optional iridescent panels (light blue/silver) on thighs, shins, sides—futuristic, sleek.
- **Cape:** Flowing, lightweight cape in matching light cream or beige. Draped over shoulders, extends to mid-calf. Same fabric as jumpsuit, billows gently at sides.
- **Necklace:** Multi-strand, layered beaded necklace. Small cylindrical or seed-like beads in olive green, dark forest green, earthy brown, dark reddish-brown, gold, black. Accents of red and yellow beads. Traditional craftsmanship, sits high on neck.
- **Bag:** Small rectangular crossbody bag. Woven material or intricate beadwork. Geometric pattern in dark green, olive green, dark brown. Olive green strap. Hangs on right hip, strap over left shoulder.
- **Shoes:** Flat, open-toed sandals. Multiple thin straps in light beige or cream crisscross over feet and around ankles (gladiatorial-style). Sole: slightly darker natural brown.

- **Pose:** Confident, open, joyful

**Variations for other characters**
- Same cultural aesthetic: futuristic, flowing, light-toned
- Individualized: different cuts, capes vs shawls, accessories, subtle colors
- Men, women, children in similar but distinct outfits

## Dome City (Exterior)

**Landscape**
- Verdant mountainous valley
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset)
- Winding river through the heart
- Terraced permaculture gardens (vibrant green)
- Arched bridges with greenery, spanning the river
- Rolling mountains in distance, atmospheric haze

**Architecture**
- Large geodesic domes clustered on valley floor and slopes
- Translucent or light-colored triangular panels
- Clear grid pattern (glass or similar)
- Varying sizes, interconnected clusters
- Modern, futuristic, eco-conscious

**Foreground elements**
- Mango tree with unripe fruit (left)
- Diverse plant life: ferns, shrubs, flowering plants
- Dark soil, grass, moss, roots, small stones

---

## Dome Interior

**Structure**
- High geodesic ceiling with clear triangular panels
- Abundant natural daylight from above
- Large open halls, multiple levels
- Open sightlines

**Materials**
- **White stone**: Pillars, lower walls, stacked rough-hewn blocks
- **Black steel**: Structural beams, railings, upper walkways
- **Mango wood**: Beams, ceiling panels, furniture, cabinetry
- Blend: rustic meets elegant (Tuscany-inspired)

**Integration with nature**
- Climbing vines (ivy) on pillars
- Planters on walkways with overflow foliage
- Plants climbing walls, trees through openings
- Daylight filtering through dome

**Furniture**
- Simple, minimal wooden lounge chairs
- Light-colored cushions
- Low wooden coffee tables

## Lighting

**Golden hour exterior**
- Sun low on horizon
- Long soft shadows
- Warm orange, yellow, soft pink
- Volumetric light beams
- Rim light on silhouettes
- Cinematic contrast

**Dome interior**
- Soft daylight through clear panels
- Dappled light among plants
- Golden highlights on stone and wood
- Gentle shadows
- Warm, serene

**Photorealistic / cinematic**
- Clear contrast between lit and shadowed areas
- Subtle volumetric light
- Shallow depth of field (f/2.0) for focus
- No flat "painted" lighting

---

## Atmosphere & Mood

- Eco-futuristic
- Warm, hopeful, serene
- Lived-in, heartwarming home
- Lively when populated (natural activities, shared moments)
- Nature deeply integrated with architecture
- No empty or staged feel

---

## Composition Notes

**Scale**
- People must be human-sized relative to architecture
- Avoid "cut out" look—integrate figures into scene
- Use depth of field to show scale (distant elements softer)

**Poses**
- Natural, everyday rather than formal portrait
- Relaxed interactions (arm around child, picking fruit)
- Variety: standing, sitting, walking paths
