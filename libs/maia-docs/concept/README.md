# Maia Concept Documentation

This directory contains comprehensive documentation of the Maia vision, organizational structure, and product ecosystem.

## Document Overview

### [00 - Terminology](./00-terminology.md)
Quick reference glossary of all key terms, concepts, and names used across the Maia ecosystem. Start here for definitions.

**Key Topics**: Core concepts, product names, organizational terms, quick definitions

---

### [01 - Foundation](./01-foundation.md)
The foundational principles that guide everything we build: our vision, mission, and core values.

**Key Topics**:
- **Vision**: Building 10x better environments for 8.1 billion humans
- **Mission**: Creating Maia City as Earth's first capital for 1.3 million citizens
- **Values**: Purpose-driven, Self-sovereignty, First-principles
- **Design Philosophy**: Human-centric, AI-native, distributed governance
- **Impact Goals**: Wealth, health, happiness

---

### [02 - Organizational Structure](./02-organizational-structure.md)
The legal entity hierarchy and governance model powering the Maia ecosystem.

**Key Topics**:
- **Entity Hierarchy**: Maia Garden e.V. → Maia Holding GmbH → Maia City GmbH
- **Maia Garden e.V.**: Brand, IP, and governance (non-profit)
- **Maia Holding GmbH**: Asset management and investment vehicle
- **Maia City GmbH**: Operating company and product delivery
- **Governance Principles**: Transparency, accountability, democratic input
- **Stakeholder Roles**: Members, shareholders, employees, users

---

### [03 - Products & Services](./03-products-and-services.md)
The four-tier product ecosystem bringing the Maia vision to market.

**Key Topics**:
- **Tier 0**: Maia Universe (Free) - Social platform for purpose-driven content
- **Tier 1**: Maia Voice (€34/month) - AI conversational assistant
- **Tier 2**: Maia Creator Academy (€233/month) - Entrepreneurship training
- **Tier 3**: Maia Founder (€987/month) - Investment stake in Maia Holding
- **Maia Vibes**: Interactive mini-applications
- **Maia Creators**: Vibe creators and ecosystem contributors
- **Product Strategy**: Entry paths, synergies, revenue model
