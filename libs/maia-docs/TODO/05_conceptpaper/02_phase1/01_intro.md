## The Goal: 100% City Simulation

We don't simulate parts of a city. **We simulate everything.**

The economic system runs completely. When you invest Hearts into a Spark, you receive SparkMinds that generate real income streams. When citizens consume electricity, payments flow. When Sparks lease land, revenue distributes. Every currency, every ownership stake, every market dynamic, every wealth creation mechanism operates exactly as it would in the physical city.

The infrastructure operates completely. Solar panels generate power. Water systems purify and distribute. Underground logistics move resources. Housing markets allocate apartments based on what people value. Energy flows, water flows, people move—all serving 1.3 million virtual citizens making real choices.

Governance happens completely. Citizens vote on Spark decisions. Resources get allocated based on collective preferences. Disputes resolve through transparent processes. Policies emerge from actual problems people face. Every governance challenge the real city will encounter gets tested first here.

Social dynamics emerge naturally. People discover how to collaborate. Wealth distributes based on contribution and ownership. Innovation happens because anyone can found a Spark. Community forms through shared stakes in collective success. We don't design these dynamics—we create conditions and let them emerge.

Environmental integration models everything. Regenerative agriculture building soil. Water systems restoring watersheds. Energy systems operating carbon-free. Every regenerative principle we claim will work gets tested against simulated reality.

## The Proof: More Wealth for All

The simulation will prove something revolutionary:

**Collective ownership creates MORE wealth than individual extraction.**

### Current System (Extractive):

Landlords collect rent from tenants. The wealth flows one direction—from renter to owner—and stays there. Corporations generate profits, but those profits concentrate with shareholders while workers receive fixed wages. Employees create value, but owners accumulate the wealth. Value flows upward, inequality grows, and the vast majority of humans spend their lives making other people wealthy.

**Result:** A tiny elite own almost everything. Everyone else struggles, working to survive rather than thriving.

### MaiaCity System (Circular):

Citizens own the cooperatives they consume from. When you pay rent, revenue flows to a Spark you co-own, and your ownership stake earns you a portion back. AI agents operate everything efficiently without extracting profit for management layers. Everyone earns from their ownership stakes, so value distributes to all participants. Revenue streams continuously back to you, creating a circular flow where your spending generates your income.

**Result:** Wealth circulates rather than concentrates. Everyone thrives together because success is shared, not hoarded.
