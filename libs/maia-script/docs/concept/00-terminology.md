# Maia Terminology - Quick Reference

## Core Concepts

### Maia Garden
The main brand, encompassing the website, social media channels, and organizational identity. Maia Garden e.V. is the democratic Brand, IP Asset Holder, and creator entity for Maia AI.

### Maia Holding GmbH
100% subsidiary of Maia Garden e.V., responsible for organizational management and asset holding for all future cities' assets, including real estate, housing, startups etc and Maia City infrastructure.

### Maia City GmbH
Formerly "Visioncreator GmbH" - the operational entity building AI-native infrastructures and platforms for the coordination of Maia City. Brings Maia AI and Maia Garden brand to market through product offerings.

### Maia AI
The artificial intelligence system powering the Maia ecosystem, designed to enhance human productivity, creativity, and sovereignty.

### Maia Vibes
Interactive mini-applications created by Maia Creators - ranging from mini-games to presentations and productivity tools - shared within the Maia Universe platform.

### Maia Creators
Self-sovereign solo-entrepreneurs who create vibes and build within the Maia ecosystem. Trained through the Maia Creator Academy.

## Product Names

### Maia Universe
Free, attention economy platform (domain: maia.garden) - a social media network focused on good news and human purpose-driven content, featuring interactive vibes.

### Maia Voice
AI voice conversational assistant service offering interactive voice capabilities with vibe integration.

### Maia Creator Academy
Interactive educational course designed to transform individuals into self-sovereign solo-entrepreneurs.

### Maia Founder
Investment tier providing real stake ownership in Maia Holding GmbH, designed for future Maia City citizens.

## Organizational Terms

### Maia Garden e.V.
Democratic non-profit organization serving as the Brand, IP Asset Holder, and creator entity. Manages governance and guardianship of all divisions.

### Democratization Principles
Core governance approach emphasizing distributed decision-making and community participation.

### First Principles
Value-driven approach to problem-solving by breaking down complex problems to their fundamental truths.

### Self-Sovereignty
Core value emphasizing individual autonomy, independence, and self-determination.
