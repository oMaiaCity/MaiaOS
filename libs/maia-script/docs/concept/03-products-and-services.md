# Products & Services

## Product Ecosystem Overview

Maia City GmbH brings four core product tiers to market, each serving different user needs while building toward the vision of self-sovereign city living.

```
Tier 0: Maia Universe (Free)
Tier 1: Maia Voice (€34/month)
Tier 2: Maia Creator Academy (€233/month)
Tier 3: Maia Founder (€987/month)
```

---

## Tier 0: Maia Universe

### Concept
A free, attention economy platform reimagining social media around good news, human purpose-driven content, and interactive experiences.

### Domain
`maia.garden`

### Key Features
- **Good News Focus**: Curated content emphasizing positive human achievements, solutions, and progress
- **Purpose-Driven Content**: Stories, ideas, and discussions centered on meaningful human endeavors
- **Interactive Vibes**: Users share not just posts, but interactive mini-applications (vibes)
- **Mind Growth**: Content and experiences designed to expand human consciousness and capability
- **Community Building**: Connect with like-minded individuals pursuing purpose and sovereignty

### Maia Vibes Ecosystem
Vibes are interactive mini-applications that can include:
- Mini-games for learning and entertainment
- Interactive presentations and storytelling
- Productivity tools and utilities
- Educational experiences
- Collaborative workspaces
- Creative expression tools

### Differentiation from Traditional Social Media
- **Quality over Virality**: No algorithmic manipulation for engagement
- **Purpose over Profit**: Free tier supports community building, not data extraction
- **Creation over Consumption**: Empowering users to build, not just scroll
- **Transparency**: Clear about how the platform operates and generates value

### User Journey
Maia Universe serves as the entry point to the Maia ecosystem, introducing users to:
1. The Maia brand and vision
2. The concept of vibes and interactive content
3. The community of purpose-driven individuals
4. Pathways to deeper engagement (Voice, Academy, Founder tiers)

## Tier 1: Maia Voice

### Pricing
€34 per month

### Concept
Conversational AI voice assistant providing natural, intelligent dialogue with integrated vibe interaction capabilities.

### Key Features
- **Conversational AI**: Natural language processing for human-like dialogue
- **Voice-First Interface**: Hands-free interaction optimized for real conversations
- **Vibe Integration**: Voice assistant can interact with and control vibes
- **Usage Allowance**: Specified number of free conversation minutes per month
- **Privacy-Preserving**: Conversations processed with user privacy as priority
- **Multi-Platform**: Available across devices (mobile, desktop, in-app)

### Use Cases
- **Personal Assistant**: Task management, reminders, scheduling
- **Learning Companion**: Educational discussions, knowledge exploration
- **Creative Partner**: Brainstorming, ideation, problem-solving
- **Vibe Navigation**: Voice control of Maia Universe vibes
- **Daily Companion**: Meaningful conversations, reflections, growth discussions

### Technical Capabilities
- Real-time voice processing
- Context-aware responses
- Integration with user's Maia ecosystem data
- Customizable personality and interaction style
- Multi-language support

### Business Model
Subscription-based with monthly fee providing:
- Specified usage minutes
- Priority access to latest AI models
- Advanced features as they're developed
- Technical support

### Key Features

  - Programming Vibes
  - Meta AI-Vibe

- **Community Access**: Network with fellow Maia Creators
- **Lifetime Learning**: Continuous content updates and skill development

### Curriculum Areas
1. **Mindset & Philosophy**
   - Self-sovereignty principles
   - Purpose-driven business thinking
   - First-principles problem-solving

2. **Technical Skills**
   - Vibe development and programming
   - AI tool mastery
   - Digital product creation
   - Platform navigation

3. **Business Fundamentals**
   - Solo-entrepreneurship models
   - Value creation and capture
   - Marketing and audience building
   - Financial sovereignty

4. **Maia Ecosystem Mastery**
   - Understanding the Maia vision
   - Navigating product ecosystem
   - Community contribution

### Outcomes
Graduates become Maia Creators capable of:
- Creating valuable vibes for the ecosystem
- Building independent, sovereign businesses
- Contributing to the Maia City vision
- Generating income through vibe creation

### Privacy-Preserving AI

## Tier 3: Maia Founder

### Pricing
€987 per month

### Concept
Investment tier providing real ownership stake in Maia Holding GmbH, designed for the 1.3 million future Maia City citizens.

### Structure
- **Monthly Investment**: €987/month commitment
- **Equity Position**: Real stake in Maia Holding GmbH
- **Asset Backing**: Investment backed by city infrastructure, real estate, and holdings
- **Long-Term Value**: Participation in asset appreciation as Maia City develops

### Benefits
1. **Ownership Stake**
   - Equity in Maia Holding GmbH
   - Proportional to investment amount
   - Potential for asset appreciation
   - Dividend rights (if applicable)

2. **Founder Status**
   - Recognition as Maia City founder
   - Priority in future city residence selection
   - Governance participation rights
   - Exclusive community access

3. **All Previous Tiers Included**
   - Maia Universe access
   - Maia Voice subscription
   - Maia Creator Academy membership
   - All premium features

4. **Founder-Exclusive Benefits**
   - Early access to new products and features
   - Direct influence on city development plans
   - Networking with other founders
   - Investment reports and transparency

### Investment Thesis
By investing as a Maia Founder:
- Participate in building Earth's first sovereign capital city
- Gain ownership in tangible assets (real estate, infrastructure)
- Join a community of 1.3 million like-minded individuals
- Position yourself for residency in Maia City
- Support the vision of better human environments

### Asset Growth Model
Investment value expected to grow through:
- Real estate appreciation as Maia City develops
- Infrastructure asset value increase
- Network effects of growing community
- Economic activity within ecosystem
- Strategic investments by Maia Holding GmbH

### Transparency & Reporting
Founders receive:
- Quarterly financial reports
- Asset portfolio updates
- Development milestone tracking
- Governance participation opportunities
- Direct communication channels

### Target Audience
Designed for individuals who:
- Align deeply with Maia values
- Have financial capacity for €987/month commitment
- Want ownership in the future of cities
- Seek long-term value creation
- Desire active participation in building Maia City

## Product Strategy & User Journey

### Entry Path
```
Maia Universe (Free)
    ↓ (discover value)
Maia Voice (€34/mo)
    ↓ (experience AI capabilities)
Maia Creator Academy (€233/mo)
    ↓ (become contributor)
Maia Founder (€987/mo)
```

### Value Proposition by Tier

**Tier 0**: "Explore the future of human connection"
**Tier 1**: "Augment your daily life with AI"
**Tier 2**: "Become a self-sovereign entrepreneur"
**Tier 3**: "Own the future of cities"

### Cross-Tier Synergies
- Maia Creators create vibes consumed in Maia Universe (free)
- Maia Voice users interact with vibes created by Maia Creators
- Founders fund infrastructure that enables the entire ecosystem
- Free tier users provide feedback improving paid products

### Pricing Rationale
- **€34**: Accessible monthly AI subscription (comparable to streaming services)
- **€233**: Serious educational investment (comparable to university courses)
- **€987**: Meaningful investment commitment (shows long-term dedication)

### Revenue Model
- **Tier 0**: Free (user acquisition, community building)
- **Tier 1**: Recurring revenue, operational costs coverage
- **Tier 2**: High-margin education product, community growth
- **Tier 3**: Capital accumulation, asset acquisition, city development
